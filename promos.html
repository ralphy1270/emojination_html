Promo Mechanics

1. This promo is open to all Filipino citizens aged 18 years old and above.

2. Watch Emojination every Saturday at 5:30 PM on TV5 and 8:00 PM on BUKO.

3. During the show, Her Emojisty Maja and the Emojination hosts will explain how home viewers can join the “Ka-Nation at Home” promo and win exciting prizes.

4. Viewers should stay tuned throughout the episode to catch the EMOJI QUESTION OF THE WEEK flashed onscreen.

5. After the episode, go to the official TV5 or BUKO Facebook page, find the EMOJI QUESTION OF THE WEEK post, and click the link provided to submit your entry.

6. Fill out the entry form with the following details:

  a. Correct answer to the EMOJI QUESTION
  b. Full Name
  c. Date of Birth
  d. Complete Address
  e. Mobile Number
  f. Email Address

7. Only one entry per person is allowed per week.

8. Submission deadline is on Monday at 12:00 noon, following the Saturday episode.

9. The Emojination team will announce the winners in the next episode. The results will also be posted on the official TV5 and Buko Facebook pages.

10. Winners will be selected via an electronic randomizer from all valid entries.

11. Winners will be contacted by the Emojination team via phone or email.

12. Five (5) winners will be selected weekly, each receiving ₱2,000 in cash.

13. Prizes will be sent via bank transfer or remittance; all transaction fees will be covered by the show.

14. Employees of TV5, Cignal TV, MediaQuest Ventures, APT Entertainment, and their subsidiaries—including their relatives up to the second degree of consanguinity or affinity—are disqualified from joining the promo.

15. Promo runs from May 31, 2025, to August 9, 2025.
